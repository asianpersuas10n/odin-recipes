Tonkatsu Sauce

Description

Tonkatsu sauce is generally served with the Japanese dish tonkatsu. However, it is a tasty sauce on its own that can be used for other dishes.

Ingredients

  • (Optional) 1 tbsp Sesame seeds
  • 1 tbsp worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp tomato sauce/ketchup
  • 1/2 tbsp brown sugar

Steps

  1. (Optional) Roast sesame seeds on low heat for a few minutes.
  2. Add all ingredients to a small bowl and mix untill completely incorperated.
