Filipino Adobo

Description

This is the most popular filipino dish. Filipino adobo is generally refered to as adobo as its not just only the most popular filipino dish its also the most popular iteration that you will find. This dish is also best prepared with rice as a side. This recipe will be using chicken (in its place you can of course substitue for any protein), and a mixture of vinegar and soy sauce as the main components. Just make sure to look in the recipe for other ingredients that will be needed.

Ingredients

  • 8 chicken thighs
  • 6 cloves of garlic crushed or minced
  • 1 tablespoon of whole black peppercorns
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup rice vinegar
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • bay leaves

Steps

  1. In a large bowl combine and mix all ingredients except for the chicken. When the marinade is made submerge all chicken thighs.
  2. Cover the bowl and refridgerate for a minimum of 30 minutes, preferably overnight, keep the marinade.
  3. In a large pot over medium-high heat, take chicken out of the marinade and into the pan without overcrowding. Sear the chicken about 3 minutes per side. cook until browned.
  4. Remove all chicken and place the marinade into the pot. Bring it to a boil then bring it to a low heat simmer.
  5. Once again submerge the chicken in the marinade then cover the pot. Simmer for 30 minutes then flip over and simmer again for another 30 minutes.
  6. Enjoy! Make sure not to eat the bay leaves and full peppercorns. I mean I can not stop you. You have all the power in this situation.
